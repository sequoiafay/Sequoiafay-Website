About Me

Sequoia is a folk singer-songwriter based in Richmond, Virginia and raised in New England. Influenced by traditional American music, her songs are lyrically inspired and delivered through imagery and storytelling.

Her style features Travis picking, melodic finger-style, and a gentle vocal texture, creating music that is both timeless and deeply personal. She is available for live performances, collaborations, and session work.
